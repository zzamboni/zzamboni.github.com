I am a computer scientist, programmer, and overall geek living in Mexico with my awesome wife Susana and our daughters Karina and Fabiola.

I work at HP Mexico as an Outsourcing Consultant. My main fields of knowledge are computer security, virtualization and configuration management.

I was born in Argentina, but have moved around all my life. When I was very young I moved to Mexico, where I lived in four different cities before moving to the U.S. to pursue my Ph.D. at Purdue University under the direction of Gene Spafford. Upon finishing my studies, my wife and I decided to go to Switzerland, where I worked at the IBM Zurich Research Lab. After eight years, we moved back to Mexico in late 2009.
