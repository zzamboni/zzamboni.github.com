Dos meses!

Apr-11-2006 By Becker

Parece increible que hace poco estábamos festejando las dos semanas de vida de Kari, y hoy ya cumple dos meses de estar con nosotros! En los pocos días de sol que ha habido últimamente, nos fuimos a pasear al lago y al parque.

It’s hard to believe that not too long ago we were celebrating Kari’s 2 weeks of age, and today we celebrate her two months! In the few sunny days we have had lately, we’ve been to the lake and to the park.
